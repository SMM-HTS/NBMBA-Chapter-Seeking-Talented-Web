About Our Chapter

Building Bridges to Success

Our chapter of the National Black MBA Association is dedicated to creating educational, wealth-building, and growth opportunities for black professionals, students, and entrepreneurs.

With over 15 years of service to our community, we've helped thousands of members advance their careers, develop leadership skills, and build meaningful professional relationships.

Our programs include mentorship initiatives, professional development workshops, networking events, and exclusive access to corporate partners looking to diversify their talent pipelines.
